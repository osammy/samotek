Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize the app to suit my needs?

Yes. Samotek local delivery and logistics app is highly customizable. You can customize the price matrix, upload your logo and set your brand colors to meet your requirements. Your way bill will show your logo, address and other information.

Can I upgrade my license anytime?

Yes. At anytime you can upgrade your license to meet your needs. However, a cost is attached to your request to upgrade at anytime. You can send an email or call us to request for an upgrade at anytime.

Can I set sales targets for my staff on the app?

Yes. The app allows you to set sales target for staff and monitor their progress on a weekly, monthly or yearly basis. Sales target report is automatically generated and can be used for staff appraisal on a monthly basis.

Do I have to pay to download this app?

No. The app is absolutely free for download. However, a paid license is required to access the full futures of the app. The process of acquiring Samotek license key is very simple and straight forward.

Is Samotek a desktop or a mobile app?

Samotek local delivery management app is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) which can currently be deployed only as a desktop app. However, we plan to develop a mobile version of the app soon, depending on our clients’ requests.

Can I use Samotek anywhere in Nigeria?

Samotek local delivery and logistics management app can be used in any part of the country to effectively and profitably manage any delivery business. Just download the app, configure it to suit your needs and deploy it to help you manage your delivery business on a day-to-day basis.
